What I'm all about.

I've spent a lot time thinking about what it means to be successful. Still don't have an answer, but I've mainly tied it down to having a clear ambition.

So far, I've mainly found passion through nanotech right now. Mai nly using it to tackle what doesn't make sense to me, from allergies, to cancer. Currently I'm trying to understand more effective methods of COVID treatment.
